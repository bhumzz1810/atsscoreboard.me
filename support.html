Common fixes

  • Analysis fails or shows an error — Reload the job posting page, then click Analyze again. If the backend is cold-starting (Heroku), wait 10 seconds and retry.
  • JD not detected correctly — Highlight the correct job description text on the page before clicking Analyze. The extension will use your highlighted text instead.
  • Side panel doesn't open — Click the ATSScoreboard icon in the Chrome toolbar. If you don't see it, click the puzzle piece icon and pin ATSScoreboard.
  • Score seems too low — Check the Parse Preview tab. If the parser couldn't read your PDF properly, try pasting the text instead.
  • AI Coach says "requires Pro" — You need an active Pro ($9/mo) or Elite ($19/mo) subscription. Click one of the plan buttons in the AI Coach tab to subscribe.

Billing & subscription

  • Cancel your subscription — Open the extension → AI Coach tab → click "Manage" → this opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you can cancel.
  • Update payment method — Same as above — the Stripe portal lets you update your card.
  • Request a refund — Email us within 7 days of the charge. Include the email address you used at checkout.
