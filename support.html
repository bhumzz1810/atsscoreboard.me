Resolve extension issues fast.

Start here for the issues people hit most often inside ATSScoreboard.

Reload the job posting page, then click Analyze again. If the backend is cold-starting, wait a few seconds and retry once.
Highlight the correct job description text before clicking Analyze. ATSScoreboard will use your highlighted selection instead of the full page.
Click the ATSScoreboard icon in the Chrome toolbar. If you do not see it, open the extensions menu and pin ATSScoreboard first.
